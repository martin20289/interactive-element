About this project×

This is my first active JavaScript project. The idea and motivation for this collection of interactive animations come from a website by Webflow called "The History of the Web". Here I am attempting to recreate some of the effects they put on their website, namely the parallax motion between objects relative to the position of the pointer on the screen, and the dynamic resizing of objects by using the mouse scroll.

To enhance the user experience, I have 3 control buttons located at the bottom of the page. The button "B" is for toggling backgrounds, button "R" is for rescaling the images to original sizes, and button "T" is for themes but is actually a tongue-in-cheek joke*. These events can also be triggered by pressing "B", "R", or "T" on the keyboard.

Last but not least, I hope you enjoy the features of this exhibition website as much as I enjoyed building them!

Cheers!
